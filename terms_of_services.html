Terms of Service

  1. Eligibility: Mavatec certificates are available to individuals and organizations worldwide. Applicants must provide complete and accurate information as part of the application and KYC (Know Your Customer) processes.
  2. Application Process: Applicants must submit the required application form with details such as name, company, email, and selected certificate. The KYC form and any supporting documentation must also be provided. All forms are sent from our email at info@mavateccorp.com.
  3. Payment: Certificate fees can be paid via credit card using our Stripe integration or through bank transfer to our US bank account. Payments are typically processed within 1-3 business days.
  4. Certificate Issuance: Once the application, KYC, and payment have been successfully processed, Mavatec will issue the certificate in PDF format and deliver it via email. Processing time is usually 1-3 business days, excluding weekends.

  9. Information Requirements: Certification is based on the information provided in the KYC form and any supporting documents requested by Mavatec. Incomplete or inaccurate information may result in application rejection.
