Steps

Below are the Steps to obtain the certificate

Certificate Acquisition Steps

Step 1: Submit Application Form

Send us your application form with key details: Name or Company Name, Email, Phone and Selected Certificate.

Step 2: Submit KYC Form

Fill out and send us the form required to receive your certificate.

Step 3: Make Payment

Complete the certificate payment via credit card or bank transfer.

Step 4: Receive Certificate

Get your official certificate in PDF format by email.


FAQ: Certificate Acquisition Process

  1. What details do I need to submit in the application form?

    You need to provide your Name or Company Name, Email, Phone, and the specific Certificate you are applying for.

  2. What is the KYC (Know Your Customer) form?

    The KYC form is an additional document we require to verify your identity and eligibility for the certificate. You'll need to fill this out and send it to us.

  3. How can I make the certificate payment?

    You can complete the payment via credit card using our Stripe integration or through a bank transfer to our US bank account.

  4. How long does it take to receive the certificate?

    Once we've processed your application, KYC form, and payment, we'll send you the certificate in PDF format. This usually takes 1-3 business days.

  5. How can I contact you if I have questions?

    You can reach us through the contact form on our website or by emailing info@mavateccorp.com.

  6. What are the benefits of becoming a certified partner?

    Paid certificates make you an authorized, certified partner. This entitles you to eco-certifications and favorable sales terms when working with us.

  7. Do you work with clients globally?

    Yes, we certify clients from any country around the world.

  8. What information is required for the certification process?

    Certification is based on the details provided in your KYC form and any supporting documents we request, such as proof of identity, documents of your company, suppliers, mining concessions, etc.

  9. What happens if my information is incomplete?

    If you don't fully provide the required information, we may have to reject your application.

  10. How long are the certificates valid for?

    All certificates are valid for 1 year. We reserve the right to revoke a certificate if the holder is found to be non-compliant.

  11. What security measures are in place?

    Each certificate has a unique QR code that links to the holder's information. This helps with quality control and fraud prevention.
